About GharHak

GharHak is a free educational resource for flat buyers, housing society members, apartment association residents, and anyone navigating housing legal issues in Maharashtra.

Most legal information is buried in complex language that ordinary people cannot understand. We believe that's unfair. GharHak translates the law into plain, honest language — so you know what you're entitled to and what to do when things go wrong.

Our content is based on Maharashtra's MRTP Act 1966, RERA 2016, MOFA 1963, UDCPR, MCS Act 1960, Maharashtra Apartment Ownership Act 1970, and real cases.

⚠️ Disclaimer — Please Read GharHak provides general educational information only. Nothing here is legal advice. Laws change — always verify current rules. For complex matters, property transactions, or court cases, consult a qualified lawyer.

When you must get a lawyer: Receiving a legal notice · Filing or fighting a court case · Signing any agreement, settlement, or conveyance deed · Large amounts of money or property at stake.
